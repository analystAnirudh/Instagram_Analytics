Strategic Recommendations

+

Based on the comprehensive data analysis, the following strategic actions are recommended:

User Engagement Strategy

1. Implement a loyalty program targeting the oldest users to increase retention
2. Launch re-engagement campaigns for inactive users with content creation incentives
3. Schedule major marketing campaigns for Thursdays and Sundays when user activity peaks

Content Strategy

1. Promote trending hashtags to brands for sponsored content opportunities
2. Highlight high-performing content like the contest winner to encourage participation
3. Develop content creation guides focusing on popular themes (smiles, beaches, parties)

Platform Integrity

1. Review and potentially remove identified bot accounts to maintain authentic engagement
2. Implement safeguards to detect and prevent future bot activity
3. Monitor average posts per user as a key health metric
